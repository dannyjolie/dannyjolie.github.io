I guess I should explain myself

I was bored. I had a couple of old websites that I never updated. I was sick and tired of Wordpress. I wanted to play with Jekyll. So I did. This whole thing is to be regarded as work in progress. I'm basically just messing about, and antiweb.no does not necessarily reflect best practices or the quality of my work when someone pays me.

The contents of this website are aimed at no one in particular. It is a place to put whatever comes to my mind. Sometimes I write about Javascript. And all that goes with it.
